What this does

Sets PSN state to "signed in" on your PS5 so you can use features that require a signed-in account. You must offline-activate the account first.

Prerequisite: Offline activation

The account must have a non-zero account ID. Use OffAct (or similar) on the PS5:

  • Download OffAct and extract to /data/homebrew/OffAct on the PS5.
  • Run it via websrv (same way you run other homebrew).
  • Complete the activation so the account is offline-activated.
OffAct (GitHub)

Steps (in order)

  1. Offline-activate the account with OffAct (see above).
  2. On the Home page, run Jailbreak and wait until Y2JB is done.
  3. Enter your PS5 IP below (or set it on Home) and click Send Account Activator.
  4. Reboot the console for the fake sign-in to apply.
